FAQ

Q: How do I share my household?
Go to Settings, copy your invite code, and share it with your housemates. They can join by entering the code in Settings > Join Household.


Q: Is my data synced across devices?
Yes. Your data syncs automatically when connected to the internet. The app also works offline and will sync when back online.


Q: How do I delete my data?
Please contact us at info@lh.tools and we will process your request.
